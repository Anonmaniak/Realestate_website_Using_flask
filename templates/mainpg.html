MASS METROPOLIS

Mass Metropolis, a residential concept of community living, is a widely spread residential project by Mass Group of Companies. Mass Metropolis is located at SG Barve Marg, CST Road, Chembur, Mumbai. It offers 1 and 2 BHK apartments and the size of the apartments is 665 to 1,085 sq. ft. The apartments are available only through the builder. Several amenities are present for the community, including a swimming pool, intercom system, children’s play area, club house, and round the clock security amongst others.
