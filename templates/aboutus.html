About Us

The Mass Group was initiated in the year 1994 with a view to serve the clients with wondrous masterpiece. The builder group binds desire with comfort to provide the clients with elegant, dazzling and superb houses. The group makes an art work which speaks about their perfection and talent in enrolling the best skilled men in the right place. The group deals in commercial as well as residential projects. Their core focus is to deliver the best in the assured time with a classy work finish. They are experts in their field and also guide their clients through various solutions in the property related topics. Mass Group have introduced many schemes and alternative for their clients to build their homes not just houses.
